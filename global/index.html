üìê ICAIRE Methodology Note

The ICAIRE Global AI Readiness Monitor aggregates data from nine major international indices and assessments. Each index uses its own methodology, scale, and coverage. ICAIRE does not alter source data but provides a unified view for cross-comparison. Where indices overlap, differences in ranking may reflect methodological variation rather than substantive disagreement. UNESCO RAM dimension scores (1-5) reflect qualitative expert assessments across five pillars. For detailed methodology, consult each source directly.

This dashboard is maintained by the International Center for AI Research and Ethics (ICAIRE), a UNESCO Category 2 Center based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Our mission is to advance ethical, inclusive AI development worldwide through research, capacity building, and policy support.
